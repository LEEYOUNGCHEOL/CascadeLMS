Born From Research. Proven in the real world.

CascadeLMS was started as a small project to simplify some aspects of teaching and has grown to a fully featured. With 6 years of usage and thousands of student users, we've learned a lot and we've contintuned to make things better.

See for yourself. Yeah, it's exciting.

We're not just software engineers, we're also academics. We think that the work we're doing is useful, even if you don't use CascadeLMS.
